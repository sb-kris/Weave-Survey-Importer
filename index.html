Format your survey for import

This app works best with structured survey text.

If you have a PDF, Word document, CSV, XLSX, rough notes, or a broad survey idea, use GPT or Claude first to clean it into the supported format.

Steps:

  1. Copy the cleanup prompt below.
  2. Open GPT or Claude.
  3. Upload your PDF, Word, CSV, or XLSX file, or paste your raw survey brief.
  4. Paste the cleanup prompt.
  5. Copy the final structured output.
  6. Paste it into this app.
  7. Click Validate Format.
  8. Create the survey.
